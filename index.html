Welcome to my portolio!

Henry Haro is an aspiring young professional pursuing a Master of Science in Geospatial Technnology at University of Washington - Tacoma. With a BA in Geography from Western Washinton University, minors in GIS and Geology, Henry looks to pursue a career where he can create cartographic products that help further our spatial understanding of the natural and human processes that shape our environment, and how those natural processes have shaped us and our history.

Take a look through my portfolio of projects completed using Geographic Information Systems including ArcMap and ENVI.
